Treatment Options Comparison

Understanding the different approaches to Gaucher disease treatment

Treatment Effectiveness & Characteristics

Enzyme Replacement Therapy (ERT)

  • ✓ Highly effective for all symptoms
  • ✓ Rapid symptom improvement
  • ✓ Extensive clinical data
  • ⚠ Requires IV infusion
  • ⚠ Higher cost
  • ✓ Suitable for all ages
  • ✓ Proven long-term safety

Substrate Reduction Therapy (SRT)

  • ✓ Oral medication
  • ✓ Convenient administration
  • ⚠ Slower symptom improvement
  • ⚠ Limited to mild-moderate cases
  • ✓ Lower cost
  • ⚠ Age restrictions apply
  • ✓ Good tolerability

Combination Therapy

  • ✓ Optimized effectiveness
  • ✓ Personalized approach
  • ✓ Reduced ERT frequency
  • ⚠ Complex monitoring
  • ⚠ Higher complexity
  • ✓ Emerging evidence
  • ✓ Future potential
