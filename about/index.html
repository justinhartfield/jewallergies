Our Mission & Vision

Transforming genetic knowledge into empowered health decisions for Jewish communities

JewAllergies.com was founded on the belief that genetic health information should be accessible, understandable, and culturally relevant. We recognize that Jewish communities face unique genetic health challenges, with higher carrier frequencies for certain genetic conditions due to historical population bottlenecks and founder effects.

Our platform bridges the gap between complex genetic science and practical health decisions, providing families with the knowledge they need to make informed choices about genetic testing, family planning, and health management. We are committed to serving all Jewish communities - Ashkenazi, Sephardic, and Mizrahi - with culturally sensitive resources and support.

Our Journey

Key milestones in building the world's most comprehensive Jewish genetic health platform

2023

Platform Launch

JewAllergies.com officially launched with comprehensive resources for Ashkenazi genetic conditions and partnerships with leading genetic counseling centers.

Early 2024

Sephardic & Mizrahi Expansion

Expanded our platform to include comprehensive resources for Sephardic and Mizrahi Jewish genetic conditions, filling a critical gap in available resources.

Mid 2024

Healthcare Provider Network

Launched our healthcare provider directory and professional resources, connecting families with qualified genetic counselors and specialists worldwide.

Late 2024

Community Stories Platform

Introduced our community stories platform, allowing families to share their genetic health journeys and support others facing similar challenges.

2025

Global Partnerships

Established partnerships with major Jewish organizations worldwide and launched multilingual resources to serve diverse Jewish communities globally.

Future

Research & Innovation

Continuing to expand our research initiatives and develop innovative tools for genetic health education and decision-making support.
